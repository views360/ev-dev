Calculator Tutorial

This tutorial will not reappear unless you select Reset All from the menu.

This tool calculates journey costs to compare EV subscription value against PAYG rates.

Note: Glowing green items guide the way.

Calculation Type

Switch between two calculation types:

  1. BREAK EVEN (default)
  2. COST REDUCTION.

Type: BREAK EVEN

Break Even (default calculation type) displays the break-even-miles value for all UK EV-charging subscription providers. This tells you how many miles you must drive using each provider's discounted rate to recoup its subscription fee.

Type: COST REDUCTION

Cost Reduction dives deeper into the specifics of your vehicle and charging habits as well as your planned journey distance to calculate potential savings.

By necessity, it needs a lot of information.

Assessing Results

After populating each field in either calculation mode, the results will be displayed in expandable sections. If you then change one or more form values, the results will update automatically.

If you are unsure what a field is asking for — and there may be several such fields — click theℹ️Yes, just like this.icon for more information.

Reminder About the Break-Even Calculation Type

The break-even distance is the number of miles you must drive to recoup a provider's monthly subscription fee. This is a product of your stated vehicle efficiency (miles/kWh) and your average expected PAYG rate when not using a subscription.

Important: the break-even distance begins when you first charge with a provider — not when you set off from home with a pre-charged battery.
