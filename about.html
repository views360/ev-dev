About This Tool

About EV Subs Compare

What This Tool Does

Deciding between a Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG) rate and a monthly subscription can be difficult without accurate, personalised data. Public charging costs vary wildly — from 40p/kWh to over 85p/kWh — and a subscription might cost £10/month but reduce your rate by 20p/kWh.

EV Subs Compare automates this calculation across all known UK subscription providers instantly, giving you a clear answer based on your own vehicle and driving habits.

How the Comparisons Work

The tool offers two calculation modes:

  • Break-Even Analysis — calculates the exact mileage required to recoup a subscription fee, based on your car's efficiency and your typical PAYG rate. Results update live as you adjust any input.
  • Trip Savings — perfect for planning a long-distance journey. Enter your route distance and see exactly how much you'd save with each provider's subscription rate versus paying as you go.

All calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to a server, and results are instant.

Coverage & Data

This tool covers UK public charging networks only. Subscription pricing data is reviewed and updated periodically to keep comparisons accurate, with the last update date shown in the settings menu.

If you spot a rate that looks out of date — or know of a provider we haven't included yet — please let us know. Reports from the community help to keep the data fresh.

Designed for Privacy

We believe in a privacy-first approach. This tool does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. We use local cookies solely to remember your vehicle settings and input values so the calculator is ready to use immediately on your next visit.

No account is required. No data leaves your browser. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
